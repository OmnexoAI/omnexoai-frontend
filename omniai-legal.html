📋 Summary

OMNEXO AI only collects the data needed to run the service. Your data is never sold. You can delete your data at any time.

1. Data We Collect
Data TypeWhat We CollectWhy
Account InfoName, email, phone numberTo create your account
Payment InfoTransaction ID, amount (card details are stored by Razorpay/Stripe — not us)For billing
Usage DataChat history, credits used, model preferencesTo improve the service
Device InfoIP address, browser type, device typeSecurity and fraud prevention
CookiesSession cookies, preference cookiesTo keep you logged in
2. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored on AWS servers in India — in line with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023 and DPDP Rules 2025. We will take your explicit consent before transferring any data outside India.

3. Who We Share Data With
  • Razorpay / Stripe — For payment processing only. We do not store card details.
  • AI Model Providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, etc.) — Only to process your queries. These providers follow their own privacy policies.
  • AWS — Cloud infrastructure provider. Data stays in India.
  • NO ONE ELSE — We never sell, rent or trade your data.
4. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023 & Rules 2025)
  • Right to Access — View your data via Settings → Privacy → Download My Data
  • Right to Correction — Update any incorrect details yourself anytime via Settings → Profile
  • Right to Erasure — Delete your account to remove all your personal data, except records we're legally required to retain (e.g. financial/transaction records under tax law) for the statutory period
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Opt out of any data processing
  • Right to Grievance — Raise a complaint at baudhsinghgautam@omnexoai.com
5. Data Retention

Data is stored while your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal/account data is permanently deleted within 30 days, and backup copies are purged within their normal backup cycle (up to 90 days). Records we're legally required to keep — like financial/transaction records for tax and audit purposes — are retained separately for the statutory period required by law.

6. Children's Privacy

OMNEXO AI is not for users under 18. This is because Indian law (DPDP Act/Rules) requires verifiable parental consent to process a minor's data, and AI model providers also restrict direct use by minors. We rely on a self-declared age confirmation at signup, and accounts found to belong to a minor will be suspended.
