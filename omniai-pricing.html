Frequently Asked Questions
Credits kya hote hain? ▾
Credits OMNI AI ki currency hain. Har AI model alag credits leta hai — DeepSeek sirf 2 credits/msg, Claude Sonnet 8 credits/msg. Aap choose kar sakte ho konsa model use karna hai!
Credits expire hote hain kya? ▾
Monthly plan ke credits monthly reset hote hain. Extra recharge kiye gaye credits 6 mahine tak valid rehte hain — expire nahi hote jaldi!
Kya main plan kabhi bhi change kar sakta hoon? ▾
Bilkul! Aap anytime upgrade ya downgrade kar sakte ho. Upgrade turant apply hota hai. Downgrade next billing cycle se lagta hai.
Payment methods kya hain? ▾
India: UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking — powered by Razorpay. International: Visa, Mastercard, PayPal — powered by Stripe. GST invoice automatically generated.
Refund policy kya hai? ▾
Pehle 7 din mein agar aap satisfied nahi hain toh full refund milega. Uske baad pro-rata refund policy apply hoti hai. support@omniai.in par contact karein.
