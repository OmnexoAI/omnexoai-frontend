Frequently asked questions
What are credits and how do they work? ▾
Credits are OMNEXO AI's currency — think of them like a prepaid mobile balance. Each AI model has a different cost per message: DeepSeek, Llama and Qwen cost just 2 credits (cheapest), Claude Sonnet costs 8 credits (most powerful). Smart Router automatically picks the cheapest model that fits your query, so your credits go further than you expect.
Do credits expire? ▾
Monthly plan: Credits are valid for the current billing month only. Unused credits expire when the month ends.

6-Month plan: Credits stay valid for the entire 6-month period. If you have 200 unused credits in month 3, they carry forward to month 4, 5 and 6. Nothing is wasted.
What is the 6-Month Price Lock? ▾
When you choose a 6-Month plan, your rate is locked for the full 6 months — even if OMNEXO AI increases prices for new subscribers during that period. You pay the same amount every month, guaranteed. Monthly plan users pay the current rate at each renewal.
Can I change my plan anytime? ▾
Monthly plans can be upgraded or cancelled anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately. Cancellations apply from the next billing cycle — you keep access until then.

6-Month plans run for the committed period. You can upgrade mid-way; downgrades apply after the 6-month period ends.
What payment methods are supported? ▾
India: UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking — via Razorpay. GST invoice generated automatically.

International: Visa, Mastercard, PayPal — via Stripe. USD pricing applied automatically.
What is the refund policy? ▾
No Refund Policy — all sales are final. Credits and plans are non-refundable once purchased, like a mobile recharge.

✅ One exception: if you paid but credits did not arrive in your wallet — automatic full refund within 3–5 days. No request needed.

✅ If OMNEXO AI is down 24+ hours (our fault) — automatic credit compensation for affected users.

💡 That is why we offer a free trial — try everything before spending anything.
What is Smart Router? ▾
Smart Router is OMNEXO AI's engine that automatically selects the best and cheapest AI model for each query. A simple question → DeepSeek (2 cr). Complex reasoning → Claude Sonnet (8 cr). Creative writing → GPT-5.5 (6 cr). You always get a great answer at the lowest credit cost — without thinking about it. You can also pick any model manually.
Is there a free trial? Do I need a credit card? ▾
Yes — free trial is available at signup. You get free credits to try all 9 AI models. No credit card required. Use the trial to test Smart Router, explore models and decide your plan — before paying anything.
